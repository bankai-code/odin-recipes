Chilli Garlic Omlette

Description

As the name suggests, this omelette is made with fiery chilli and piquant garlic flavours. If you are someone with spicy taste palettes, this recipe is a worthwhile attempt.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • To taste Red Chilli Powder
  • Salt To Taste
  • 3-4 Cloves Garlic (Chopped)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Break two eggs in a glass, whisk it properly.
  2. Once done add chopped garlic, salt, chilli flakes and red chilli powder.
  3. Mix everything again.
  4. Heat a dollop of butter in a pan, pour the whisked egg batter, flip and cook the omelette from both sides.
  5. You can also add a slice of cheese to give it a balanced taste.
